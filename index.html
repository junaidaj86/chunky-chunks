FAQ

How should I store the desserts?

Refrigerate tiramisu and cheesecakes. Cookies and brownies keep at room temp in an airtight container.

What is the shelf life?

Tiramisu & cheesecakes: 2–3 days chilled. Cookies & brownies: 3–5 days in airtight storage.

Allergen info?

Nuts, gluten, and dairy may be present. Ask for details prior to ordering.

Lead time?

Most orders require 24–48 hours. Larger/custom orders may require longer.

Can you customize?

Yes — flavor tweaks and decorations on request (subject to feasibility).
